Guiding principles

• data look better naked

• Internal consistency is crucial

• Advice from NYT Deputy Graphics Director Archie Tse:

  • If you make the reader click or do anything other than scroll, something spectacular has to happen
  • If you make a tooltip or rollover, assume no one will see it. If content is important for readers to see, don't hide it
  • When deciding whether to make something interactive, remember that getting it to work on all platforms is expensive
